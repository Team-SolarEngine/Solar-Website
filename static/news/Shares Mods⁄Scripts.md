# Sharing mods and scripts is now available!
As the title says, sharing your mods and scripts to the website is now available! How did we make this? I do not know... Code go magic and code works. Monkey brain don't know how working, but monkey brain happy.

## How do I share my mods?
For now, you can only send your repositories through our [discord server](https://discord.gg/RaHmP5fgyA). But, currently, the maintainer of the website ( [Daveberry](https://codedave.pages.dev/) ) is planning to add that!

## What is not allowed to share?
As per usual, we have boundaries.

### General boundaries
- NSFW (gore, sex, whatever is related to NSFW.)
- Major/Tragic events (9/11, and whatnot) in the world
- Mod that's not even made in Solar Engine
- Submitting something that's not related to your username, and whatnot.

### Interacting with system
You ARE allowed to make and submit mods/scripts that can interact with the user's computer.

**BUT PLEASE**; make sure it's not anything severe, and make it LOUD AND CLEAR that it WILL interact with your computer.

- You cannot make mods that are a virus.
  - This means deleting a highly important folder in the user's system, injecting a randsomware, infostealer, and whatnot.
- Shutting down, restarting, putting the computer to sleep IS allowed. But;
  - Be VERY clear in the mod that it will shut down their computer.
  - You will make a setting that disable shutdowns.
- Make your mod/script open source.
  - If you submit a mod/script that can interact with the computer BUT it's not open source, we will gladly deny it without any hesitation.
- Anything with interacting with files (such as creating, reading, deleting).
  - Should be alerted when the user first plays the mod/script.
  - Should NOT be the user's files or the system's files.
- Self destruct mods/scripts are allowed.
  - That means the game automatically deletes itself. Not the whole system deleting itself.
